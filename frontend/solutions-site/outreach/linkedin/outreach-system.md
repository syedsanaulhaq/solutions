# LinkedIn Outreach System — HostingOcean Solutions

UK client acquisition via LinkedIn. All messages are conversational and non-salesy — the goal of each touch is a reply, not a close.

---

## 1. Connection Request Message

*(500 character max — must fit in LinkedIn's connection note field)*

### Variant A — Service-specific (AI Chatbot)

```
Hi [First Name], I work with companies that are looking at AI to reduce support load — RAG-powered chatbots grounded in their own documentation rather than generic AI tools. Noticed [Company] is in [sector] and thought it might be relevant. Happy to connect.
```

### Variant B — Service-specific (LMS)

```
Hi [First Name], I work with training providers building custom LMS platforms — particularly where Moodle has hit its limits or multi-tenancy is needed. Saw [Company]'s work in [sector] and thought there might be a connection. Happy to connect.
```

### Variant C — Service-specific (Automation)

```
Hi [First Name], I help companies map and automate the manual processes eating their operations team's time. Saw [Company] is scaling in [sector] — often the right moment for this. Happy to connect.
```

### Variant D — General / Cold

```
Hi [First Name], I build custom software for UK businesses — AI chatbots, LMS platforms, and automation systems. Came across your profile while looking at [sector] and thought it worth connecting. No agenda, just expanding my network in the space.
```

## 2. First Message After Connection Accepted

*(Send 2–3 days after connection is accepted. Do not pitch on acceptance day.)*

### Variant A — Soft opener with value

```
Hi [First Name],

Thanks for connecting.

I noticed [Company] [relevant observation — is in X sector / recently expanded / uses Y tool]. 

We've been doing a lot of work with [similar company type] recently — particularly around [specific pain point: knowledge base chatbots / LMS migrations / workflow automation].

One thing that tends to come up: [brief insight relevant to their industry — e.g., "most training providers we speak to have hit the ceiling of what Moodle can do once they need multi-tenancy or custom reporting"].

Not sure if that's on your radar, but happy to share what we've been seeing if useful.

[Your name]
HostingOcean Solutions
```

### Variant B — Direct but low-pressure

```
Hi [First Name],

Thanks for connecting.

Quick one — we specialise in [AI Chatbot / LMS / Automation] development for [UK SMEs / enterprises / training providers]. I won't assume it's relevant to where [Company] is right now, but if it is — or if you know someone who's evaluating this kind of project — I'd be glad to have a quick call.

Either way, good to be connected.

[Your name]
```

### Variant C — Content share opener

```
Hi [First Name],

Thanks for accepting — hope things are going well at [Company].

I wrote a short piece on [relevant topic — e.g., "when to build a custom LMS vs stick with Moodle" / "what makes RAG chatbots different from generic AI tools"] that might be useful given your background in [sector].

Happy to share the link if you'd like it — or if [specific pain point] is something on your radar, I'm always happy to compare notes.

[Your name]
```

Following up with a slightly different angle — we built a pricing calculator for custom software projects that gives an instant budget range without a call: https://solutions.hostingocean.co.uk/pricing-calculator

## 4. Response to Interested Reply

*(When a prospect replies with interest but no specific ask)*

```
Hi [First Name],

Great — glad it's relevant.

A few quick questions to make sure I'm pointing you in the right direction:

1. Is this a new build or a migration/replacement of something existing?
2. Rough timeline — is this something you're looking to move on in the next 3–6 months, or still early stage?
3. Any specific constraints I should know about upfront (budget range, data residency, existing tech stack)?

No obligation — just helps me give you something useful rather than a generic overview.

[Your name]
```

## 8. Weekly Outreach Cadence

| Day | Activity | Target |
|---|---|---|
| Monday | Send 15–20 connection requests | New prospects from saved searches |
| Tuesday | Follow up on accepted connections (2–3 days old) | Anyone who accepted Mon–Thu last week |
| Wednesday | Reply to any responses | — |
| Thursday | Send 15–20 connection requests | Second batch |
| Friday | Review accepted/response rate, refine messaging | — |

**Volume targets (per week):**
- Connection requests sent: 30–40
- Accepted (target 30–40% = 10–16)
- First messages sent: 10–16
- Replies received (target 20–30% = 2–5)
- Discovery calls booked (target 40–50% of replies = 1–2/week)

**Monthly pipeline from outreach alone:** 4–8 discovery calls → 1–3 proposals → 0–1 project starts
