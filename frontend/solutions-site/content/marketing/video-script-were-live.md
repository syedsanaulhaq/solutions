## Production Notes

### Equipment (minimal setup)
- Camera: smartphone at eye level (or webcam at 1080p)
- Lighting: face a window, or use a ring light behind the camera
- Background: clean, minimal — bookshelf or plain wall preferred
- Audio: external microphone if available (Rode NT-USB or similar). Built-in laptop mic is acceptable for LinkedIn but not for YouTube

### Screen recording segments (if using)
- Record at 1920×1080
- Use the actual live site: solutions.hostingocean.co.uk
- Highlight the pricing calculator, service pages, and quote form
- Keep cursor movements slow and deliberate

### Editing
- Captions: add auto-generated captions and review before posting (LinkedIn plays silently by default)
- Intro music: optional, 2–3 seconds max, fade out
- End card: static slide — "solutions.hostingocean.co.uk" + logo

### Platform-specific versions

| Platform | Length | Format |
|---|---|---|
| LinkedIn | 60–90 sec | Square (1:1) or landscape (16:9) |
| YouTube | 3–4 min | Landscape (16:9) with thumbnail |
| Website (optional embed) | 3–4 min | Landscape, no autoplay |
| Instagram Reels / TikTok | 30–45 sec | Vertical (9:16), extract key hook |

### Posting cadence
- LinkedIn: post with the short-form version on launch day
- YouTube: post the long-form version within 1 week of launch
- Pin the LinkedIn video to your profile for 30 days
