## HostingOcean Solutions Launches Custom AI Chatbot, LMS, and Automation Platform for UK Businesses

*Specialist custom software studio opens, offering fixed-price development of AI-powered knowledge base chatbots, learning management systems, and workflow automation for SMEs and enterprises*

---

**[London, UK — April 2026]** — HostingOcean Solutions today announced the launch of its custom software development platform at [solutions.hostingocean.co.uk](https://solutions.hostingocean.co.uk), offering UK businesses specialist development services across three high-demand technology categories: AI Chatbot Development, LMS (Learning Management System) development, and Automation & Integrations.

The platform launches with a full suite of managed SaaS tiers alongside bespoke development services for organisations whose requirements exceed off-the-shelf capabilities. All services are delivered at fixed prices with written proposals produced within 48 hours of a client brief.

---

### Addressing a Growing Gap in the UK Custom Software Market

UK businesses are increasingly outgrowing the limitations of generic SaaS platforms, particularly in knowledge management, learning delivery, and workflow automation. HostingOcean Solutions positions itself as the specialist partner for organisations that need software built to their exact requirements — with full source code ownership and no ongoing vendor dependency.

"The clients we work with have typically already tried the off-the-shelf route," said a spokesperson for HostingOcean Solutions. "They hit the edge of what a platform can do — whether that's compliance requirements, integration complexity, or scale — and they need something built properly. We scope it clearly, price it transparently, and deliver it on time."

---

### Three Core Service Platforms

**AI Chatbot Development**

HostingOcean Solutions builds Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) knowledge base chatbots — AI assistants that answer exclusively from a client's own documentation, with source citations on every response. The service addresses a critical limitation of generic AI tools: hallucinated answers drawn from general training data rather than accurate, source-verified responses from company content.

The platform includes enterprise security features (SSO/SAML, private cloud deployment, data residency), human escalation routing, CRM and helpdesk integrations, and a managed knowledge base admin interface. A SaaS tier starts at £299/month; custom builds start from £8,000.

**LMS Builder**

The LMS Builder platform offers custom learning management system development for training providers, enterprises, and commercial e-learning product companies. Unlike hosted LMS platforms, HostingOcean Solutions builds systems designed around the client's specific instructional design, compliance reporting requirements, and user experience — with full multi-tenancy, white-label branding, SCORM and xAPI support, and HRIS/SSO integration.

A managed SaaS tier starts at £199/month. Custom builds for commercial LMS products are also available.

**Automation & Integrations**

The Automation platform delivers workflow automation across business functions including lead management, finance operations, HR processes, and reporting. Workflows are built on n8n, Make, or custom Node.js depending on technical requirements and deployment preferences, with self-hosted options available for organisations with data sovereignty requirements.

Projects begin with a workflow audit that identifies and quantifies automation opportunities before any development starts. A SaaS tier starts at £149/month.

### Transparent Pricing and Fixed-Price Delivery Model

HostingOcean Solutions operates on a fixed-price delivery model — a deliberate departure from the time-and-materials billing common in the custom development market. Clients receive written proposals with locked prices based on a clear scope before development begins.

An interactive pricing calculator is available at [solutions.hostingocean.co.uk/pricing-calculator](https://solutions.hostingocean.co.uk/pricing-calculator), allowing businesses to generate an instant budget range for their project in under two minutes without submitting contact details.

---

### Technical Capabilities

All projects are delivered using a modern, maintainable technology stack: Next.js 15 (App Router) for frontend applications, Node.js for API development, PostgreSQL for data storage, and PM2 on self-hosted VPS infrastructure. Clients receive full source code ownership, deployment documentation, and a 30-day post-launch support window as standard.
