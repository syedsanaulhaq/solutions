## Usage Notes

- All copy uses UK English (organisation, behaviour, licence as noun)
- CTA buttons: primary = blue (`#2563EB`), secondary = ghost/outline
- Quote CTAs should always include the service prefill parameter where context allows (`?service=...`)
- "Free" appears where the consultation/proposal genuinely has no cost (true — it does not)
- Avoid "Book a call" — use "Get a quote" or "Request a proposal" per brand voice
