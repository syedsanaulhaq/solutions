### Core Features Copy

**Feature 1: RAG Architecture**  
*Your knowledge. Accurate answers.*  
We index your documents, policies, product manuals, and FAQs into a vector database. Every user query is matched against your content before the LLM responds. No hallucinations — answers come from your data or the chatbot says it doesn't know.

**Feature 2: Source Citations**  
*Every answer shows its source.*  
Users can see exactly which document each answer came from. This builds trust, reduces second-guessing, and makes the chatbot auditable. Critical for compliance-sensitive industries.

**Feature 3: Human Escalation**  
*Seamless handoff to your team.*  
When a query falls outside the knowledge base's confidence threshold, or when a user explicitly requests a human, the system routes to your support channel — live chat, email, or ticketing system. No dead ends.

**Feature 4: Admin Knowledge Base Management**  
*You control the content.*  
Add, update, or remove documents through a clean admin interface. Changes go live immediately — no redeployment required. Upload PDFs, Word docs, web pages, or structured data.

**Feature 5: CRM and Helpdesk Integration**  
*Connected to your existing stack.*  
Pre-built integrations with HubSpot, Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk, and Salesforce. Conversation summaries and lead data flow to your CRM automatically. Escalations create tickets.

**Feature 6: Analytics Dashboard**  
*Understand what your users are asking.*  
See top queries, unanswered questions, escalation rate, and user satisfaction scores. Use the data to improve your knowledge base and identify product gaps.

**Feature 7: Enterprise Security**  
*For organisations where security is non-negotiable.*  
SSO/SAML, private cloud deployment, data residency options, SOC 2-ready infrastructure, role-based access control. Available on the Enterprise tier.

**Feature 8: Custom Branding**  
*Your widget. Your brand.*  
White-label the chat widget with your logo, colours, and tone of voice guidelines. Deploy on your website, customer portal, or internal tools.

### FAQ — AI Chatbot

**Q: How long does deployment take?**  
A: The Starter and Professional SaaS tiers deploy in 2–3 weeks after onboarding. Custom builds take 8–14 weeks depending on integration complexity and knowledge base preparation.

**Q: How do you prevent hallucinations?**  
A: Our RAG architecture retrieves relevant documents before generating any response. The LLM cannot draw on general knowledge — it is constrained to your content. For queries outside the knowledge base, the system returns a configured "I don't know" response and offers escalation.

**Q: What file formats can I upload to the knowledge base?**  
A: PDF, Word (.docx), plain text (.txt), Markdown, CSV, and web URLs. We also support structured data ingestion via API for dynamic content that changes frequently.

**Q: Who owns the data?**  
A: You do. Your knowledge base documents, conversation logs, and user data are yours. We never use client data for model training.

**Q: Can it integrate with our existing helpdesk?**  
A: Yes. Professional and Enterprise tiers include pre-built integrations for Zendesk, Intercom, Freshdesk, and HubSpot. Custom integrations are available on Enterprise.

**Q: Is this GDPR compliant?**  
A: Yes. We hold a Data Processing Agreement and operate on GDPR-compliant infrastructure. Enterprise tier customers can specify EU or UK data residency.

**Q: What happens when the chatbot doesn't know the answer?**  
A: It provides a configured fallback response (e.g., "I don't have information on that — let me connect you with the team") and triggers your configured escalation path. It will never fabricate an answer.

### FAQ — Automation Platform

**Q: How do you decide what to automate first?**  
A: We start with a workflow audit — a structured review of your current manual processes, time cost, and error rate. We rank automation opportunities by ROI (time saved × hourly cost of the person doing it, minus build cost). The highest-ROI workflows go first.

**Q: What tools do you build automations with?**  
A: Primarily n8n (self-hosted or cloud), Make (Integromat), and custom Node.js for complex logic. The choice depends on your volume, infrastructure preferences, and whether you want the automation platform on your servers.

**Q: Do we need to know how to code to manage automations?**  
A: No. Standard workflows built in n8n or Make are managed through a visual editor. Your team can adjust trigger conditions, update field mappings, and enable/disable workflows without writing code. Custom code nodes require developer access.

**Q: What happens when an automation fails?**  
A: Every workflow includes error handling and retry logic. On failure, you receive an alert via Slack or email with the specific error. We monitor all production workflows and respond to failures during business hours (Professional) or 24/7 for critical systems (Enterprise).

**Q: Can automations handle exceptions and edge cases?**  
A: Yes. Good automation design accounts for the 20% of cases that don't follow the standard path. We map exception handling during the workflow design phase — what should happen when a record is missing, a value is unexpected, or a third-party API returns an error.

**Q: How long does it take to build an automation?**  
A: Simple single-trigger workflows (e.g., form submission → CRM entry → Slack notification): 1–2 days. Complex multi-step workflows with conditional logic and multiple system integrations: 2–4 weeks. Full automation programmes covering multiple departments: 4–12 weeks.

**Q: Is the automation infrastructure on our servers or yours?**  
A: Your choice. We can self-host n8n on your VPS or private cloud (Enterprise tier), or run it on our managed infrastructure. Self-hosted gives you full data control and no per-execution limits.
