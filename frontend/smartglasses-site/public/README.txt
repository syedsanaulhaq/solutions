Drop your payment QR code image here named exactly:

    payment-qr.png

It will appear automatically on the Checkout page under the
"Bank Transfer" option. To change the filename or hide the QR,
edit `bankTransfer.qrImage` in src/lib/site.ts.

You can also put a demo video here, e.g. videos/demo.mp4, and set
`demoVideo.mp4Url` (or `demoVideo.youtubeId`) in src/lib/site.ts.
